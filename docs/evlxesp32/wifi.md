# Wi-Fi Configuration

This guide explains how to connect **E-VLXESP32** to your local Wi-Fi network.

!!! important
    Before installing, make sure the signal strength is good at the chosen location.
    The **E-VLXESP32** should be placed where it has a strong Wi-Fi connection and can reliably control the **VELUX®** motorized skylight window

When your **E-VLXESP32** powers on for the first time, it creates a Wi-Fi hotspot:

Look for the hotspot:

    SSID: E-VLXESP32 XXXXXX Hotspot
    Password: PQvB!h7@

## Connect and configure ##

Connect to the **E-VLXESP32** hotspot using your phone or laptop.
When the setup page opens automatically, select your home **Wi-Fi** network and enter the password.
The device will restart and connect to your network.

## If the captive portal doesn't open ##

Open a browser and go to http://192.168.4.1 and configure your **Wi-Fi** from there.
